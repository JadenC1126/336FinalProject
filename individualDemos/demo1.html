Demo 1: Neo's Choice

Keyboard controls:

  • w,a,s,d - move forward, left, backward, right
  • r,f - move up, down
  • i,j,k,l - look up, left, down, right
  • O - look at origin
  • p - place camera at origin
  • S,W - grow, shrink fov
  • I,J,K,L - orbit up, left, down, right
